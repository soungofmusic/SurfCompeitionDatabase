About

The World Surf League (WSL) has just started to formalize a circuit of competitions to determine not only who is the best surfer at a specific competition, but how each surfer is ranked overall in the world. To date, there has been no attempt to determine the world's best surfer. As such, there is currently no centralized site to see rankings and scores for competitions held worldwide. The eventual goal is to be able to surface this information to the estimated 20 - 35 Million Surfers across the globe.
